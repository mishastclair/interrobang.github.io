# Host Your Own Interrobang Night

Want to run trivia at home, online, or with your gaming friends on Discord?

Here’s how:

### 1. Play the podcast  
Just hit play — rounds are timed so you can pause naturally.

### 2. Download the answer sheets  
Each episode has its own printable PDF.

### 3. (Optional) Use our web form  
A simple Google Form works for online/Discord groups.

### 4. Support the show on Patreon  
Get full .pptx decks, themes, and party packs.
